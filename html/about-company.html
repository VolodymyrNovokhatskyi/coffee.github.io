About company

Our Company developed from Italian Trade Concern, SMM S.R.L. Once upon a time we just had a thought that supplying coffee products only would be great. So we concentrated on a new performance division focused on that goal.

SMM offers you a huge catalog of well-known coffee brands, flexible discount system bundled with perfect quality products as well as favorable partnership conditions.

Are you looking for a profitable and reliable partner? Contact us: we will answer all your questions!

Our story

SMM’s story began in Milan at the dawn of 2011. We gathered best professionals in their field who wanted to evolve their own unique creation in wholesale coffee trade industry. At that time the Сompany was just starting to supply foods, but we were looking for more new partners. Thus, the beneficial partnerships brought SMM its well-deserved fame.

Number of contracts was growing every year, so the Company was developing fast. Thus, focus shifted on exploring new horizons to provide our customers with best possible service range.

SMM apart from supplying wholesale coffee is engaged in analytics, logistics, and warehousing. Company solves issues related to international trade as well as marketing research. In addition, our partners can freely offer for considering any suggestions they have!
